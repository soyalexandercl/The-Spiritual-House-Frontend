SPIRITUAL SHARING

(From you to the universe)

Spiritual Sharing is the practice of openly expressing your inner experiences, insights, emotions, and intuitive wisdom in a safe, compassionate space.
It involves speaking from the heart about your spiritual journey your growth, challenges, awakenings, and questions while being fully present and authentic.

SPIRITUAL RECEIVING

(From the universe to you)

Spiritual Receiving is the practice of opening yourself mind, heart, and intuition to receive guidance, energy, insight, healing, or wisdom from a higher source, your inner self, or the present moment.
It is an active state of receptive awareness, where you allow rather than force.

RELAXATION, MEDITATION OR INTUITIVE MOVEMENT

Duración 20 minutos

RELAXATION

Release tension, connect to the body, open intuition. It goes beyond physical rest and invites you to release mental tension, emotional weight, and energetic blocks, allowing your inner being to reconnect with peace, clarity, and higher awareness.

MEDITATION

State of deep inner calm where the mind, body, and soul enter harmony.
Conscious practice of connecting with your inner self, your higher awareness, and the divine or universal energy.

INTUITIVE MOVEMENT

Release stuck emotions through motion. “Let the energy flow out of you and through you.”
